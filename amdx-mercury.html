Model Thesis
Catch Monday or Tuesday expansion by entering end-of-day on a PDH or PDL sweep using the 24H timeframe. When you see disagreement at a major level on HTF, enter immediately on the sweep — no need to wait for a breaker/FVG retest. When there's agreement for direction, set alerts and wait for a better entry on a breaker or FVG. Expansion comes on X, M & D days in the AMDX cycle.
Entry Checklist
1
HTF Context
Check Weekly & Monthly A/X classification. Is this ticker accumulating? What phase are we in on the quarterly AMDX cycle? Know the HTF bias before looking at the 24H chart.
✦ W+M Accumulating = strongest setup
✦ Look for X, M, or D days for expansion
2
Identify PDH / PDL on 24H TF
Mark the Prior Day High (PDH) and Prior Day Low (PDL) on the 24-hour timeframe. Also note the 0.5 equilibrium level (midpoint of the prior day range).
✦ PDH = Buy-side liquidity (stops above)
✦ PDL = Sell-side liquidity (stops below)
3
Wait for the Sweep
Wait for price to sweep PDH or PDL — taking out the liquidity sitting at those levels. This is the manipulation phase. The sweep should happen on Monday or early Tuesday for optimal expansion timing.
✦ PDL sweep → look for long (bullish)
✦ PDH sweep → look for short (bearish)
4
Entry Decision
Disagreement at HTF level? → Enter immediately on the sweep. No need to wait for retest.

Agreement for direction? → Set alert. Wait for breaker or FVG entry for better R:R. Price will give you a better entry.
✦ End-of-day entries = just hop in
✦ Target: 10+ swings per week for data
